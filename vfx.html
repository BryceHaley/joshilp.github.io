VFX Reel

The shots shown in this VFX reel are only a handful of the shots I've have worked on. This reel highlights interesting shots that involved Paint and Rotoscoping. An area that cannot be shown is my Technical Artist experience, called Extraction.

Rotoscoping

Roto involved a lot of tracing of objects in order to create precise masks. These often involved the use of various techniques to get accurate movement and precision.

Paint

Paint involved camera tracking, projections, plate restoration, color correction, and rig/marker/wire removal.

Extraction

Extraction is the processes of taking a Nuke script and breaking it down for a stereo pipeline. This could mean recreating whole scripts, fixing major technical issues, writing python code, and developing tools and python scripts to solve problems. Working in this area is where I developed an appreciating for programming and computer science, which is why I persued a second degree in this field.
